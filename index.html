Hello! My name is Rebeacca Modayil and I am a web developer!

If you met me about 6 months ago, I would have never imagined introducing myself as a web developer. I spent the last 4 years at Temple University pursuing a marketing degree because I wanted a career that allowed me to be creative, innovative, and to think outside the box. I gained lots of experience through internships and freelance work which helped me grow as a young professional. However, it didn’t dawn to me until my senior year of college that I really enjoyed the digital side of marketing. Interning as a National Digital Marketer with Subaru of America gave me the opportunity to work with web developers to fix bugs and manage web content that contributed to a flawless user experience. Towards my final semester, I started my job search to kick off my career, but to be honest, many of the marketing roles I found didn't really have the qualities I was looking for.

That’s when I decided to turn to web development. It was a few weeks before graduation when I decided to enroll in University of Pennsylvania’s Coding Bootcamp. It was a risky decision, but — boy oh boy — I am glad I took the chance! Learning full stack development opened my eyes to the possibilities of what one can create with just a few lines of code (I particularly enjoy front-end design). I am excited to see what the future entails as I continue to perfect my craft!
